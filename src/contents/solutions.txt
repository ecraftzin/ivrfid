RFID SOLUTIONS
An RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) based management system is a technology solution that uses RFID tags to track and manage assets and inventory items in real-time. RFID tags are small electronic devices with unique identifiers that can communicate wirelessly with RFID readers or scanners.
RFID solutions offer significant benefits in terms of efficiency, accuracy, and visibility throughout the supply chain. They empower organizations to make data-driven decisions, reduce operational costs, and enhance the overall performance of their assets and inventory management processes.
RFID systems are widely applied in industries like retail, logistics, healthcare, manufacturing, and security to improve efficiency, reduce losses, and enhance visibility.

BLE tracking is a wireless technology that uses small, battery-powered beacons and sensors to locate and monitor assets in real-time. 

BLE Asset Tracking
BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) asset tracking is a wireless technology ideal for indoor, real-time monitoring of equipment, inventory, and tools. It balances cost, energy efficiency, and location accuracy for assets within a building or a defined area.
BLE based asset tracking provide Real-time monitoring of assets such as inventory, equipment, and vehicles, improving efficiency and security.
The system works by attaching small, battery-powered tags to assets or placing fixed beacons in an environment. These devices broadcast signals that are picked up by receivers (like gateways or smartphones) and sent to our cloud platform for you to view on a dashboard or map.
